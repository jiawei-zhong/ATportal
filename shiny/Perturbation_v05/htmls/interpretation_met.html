The first tab visualizes the transcriptome data obtained by RNA seq analysis of Metformin treated adipocytes in a heatmap. Hereby either the absolute abundance across included individuals (allowing comparison across genes) or the z-score normalized mean abundance (default, allows comparison across cell types for multiple genes) can be shown. Clustering, as well as colorization, can be changed under Customization in the left sidebar. The Volcano tab shows all available data points, highlighting and labeling the provided genes of interest. This facilitates a comparison to the complete data set. The Table tab allows to download the results of the visualized data.

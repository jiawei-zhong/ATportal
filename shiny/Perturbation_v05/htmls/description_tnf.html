TNF

Stimulation of in vitro differentiated adipocytes (hMADS or SGBS) with TNF. For hMADs, data is available for three replicates in each condition obtained [...] sequencing, for SGBS RNA sequencing data is available for three TNF treated replicates (10ng/ml) and 12 controls. For more information, please see the 'Details' tab. Outputs currently include heatmaps, volcano plots and summary table for one or multiple genes. More information on processing and interpretation can be found under the 'Interpretation' tab.

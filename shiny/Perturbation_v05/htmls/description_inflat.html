Inflammed Adipose Tissue

Stimulation of in vitro differentiated adipocytes (SGBS) with conditioned media from omental adipose tissue explants treated with macrophage (LPS) and T-cell (anti-CD3/28) stimulants for 24 or 48 hours. RNA sequencing data is available for three cases and three controls. For more information, please see the Details tab. Outputs currently include heatmaps, volcano plots and summary table for one or multiple genes. More information on processing and interpretation can be found under the Interpretation tab.

Insulin

Adipose tissue in vivo insulins stimulation data obtained before and after an hyperinsulinemic-euglycemic clamp in subjects living without obesity or stimulation of in vitro differentiated adipocytes (SGBS) with insulin. For the in vivo data, CAGE-sequencing data is available for 19 subjects, for SGBS cells, RNA sequencing data is available for three insulin treated replicates (100 mM) and 12 controls. For more information, please see the Details tab. Outputs currently include heatmaps, volcano plots and summary table for one or multiple genes. More information on processing and interpretation can be found under the Interpretation tab.

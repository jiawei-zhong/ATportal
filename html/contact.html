The Ryden-Mejhert Lab

The Mikael Rydén & Niklas Mejhert studies multiple features of the human white adipocyte, in particular lipid and carbohydrate metabolism, energy storage capacity and immunometabolism. The lab is part of the Endocrinology Unit of the Department of Medicine Hudinge, at Karolinska Institutet.

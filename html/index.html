Module I: Clinical

This module compiles WAT transcriptome data of over 3000 subjects from XX cross-sectional cohorts to allow a detailed look at clinical correlations (e.g., BMI, HOMA, sex differences) within and across studies. Two longitudinal cohorts with 2- and 5-years data after bariatric surgery provide a weight-loss perspective. Additionally, we provide novel proteome data for 100 subjects.

This module contains transcriptomic data detailing the effects of different perturbations on gene expression in human adipocytes.
The following user-defined queries can be interrogated in this module:
External perturbations: explore transcript levels in adipocytes following external perturbations such as hypoxia and TNF-a incubations.
Genetic perturbations: study transcriptional changes in adipocytes following depletion of individual genes.
